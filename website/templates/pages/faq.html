Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our roofing services

Inspections & Quotes

How does the free inspection work?

We arrange a convenient time to visit your property and conduct a thorough roof assessment. You will receive a detailed written quote with no obligation to proceed. There is never any pressure — the inspection is genuinely free.

How long does an inspection take?

Most inspections take between 30 minutes and 1 hour, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. We will also inspect gutters, chimneys, and flashings where accessible.

When will I receive my quote?

We aim to provide your detailed written quote within 24–48 hours of the inspection. Complex projects may take slightly longer as we source specific material pricing.

Payments & Warranty

What does "pay on completion" mean?

You do not pay anything upfront. Once the work is completed to the agreed specification and you are satisfied with the result, only then do we invoice you. This ensures complete alignment between our work and your expectations.

What is covered by the 5-year warranty?

Our 5-year workmanship warranty covers any defects arising from our installation or repair work. In addition, the roofing materials we use carry their own manufacturer warranties — we will provide full documentation for both.
