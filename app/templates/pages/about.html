Your Trusted Glasgow Roofing Specialist

Glasgow Roofmasters is a roofing specialist providing all types of roofing and sheet metal work for customers throughout Scotland. Based at 236 Sauchiehall Street in the heart of Glasgow, we cater for a wide range of clients: private individuals, businesses, local authorities and public bodies.

We are equally competent and equipped to deal with small and large contracts, from a single chimney repair to full commercial roof installations.

Quality & Compliance

All of our work meets the requirements laid down in the Scottish Building Standards, which covers all materials, construction methods, safety procedures and energy efficiency issues as per the Building (Scotland) Regulations 2004 and the Technical Handbooks. We possess all relevant accreditations, approvals, etc. as per Scottish building regulations.

Environmental Responsibility

We consider environmental issues very carefully. All waste is properly contained, and where possible and practical, waste materials are re-used. Any material unable to be re-used or recycled is disposed of in an ecologically safe manner.
